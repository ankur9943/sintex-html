Sustainability Overview

A Commitment to a Greener Tomorrow

At Sintex Yarns, sustainability is a cornerstone of our operations, shaping every thread we produce. By embedding environmentally friendly practices, ethical labor standards, and innovative resource management into our processes, we aim to create a positive and lasting impact on society and the planet.

Green Initiatives to Reduce Carbon Footprint

We have adopted the 3R principle—Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle, ensuring a sustainable future through advanced technology and eco-conscious practices. Our energy-efficient plant and processes underscore our commitment to reducing our carbon footprint.

Power Optimization

  • Energy-efficient operations save 6000 kWh/day or 2.1 million kWh/year
  • Result: Annual reduction of 1836 tons of CO₂ (at 0.85 kg CO₂/kWh)

Biomass Fuel Boilers

  • Run on agri-waste like groundnut shells
  • Offset over 14,040 tons/year of CO₂ emissions

Heat Recovery Systems

  • Recover 40% of waste heat
  • Prevent 61 tons/month of CO₂ emissions

Plastic Waste Recycling

  • Over 384 MT of plastic waste recycled annually
  • In full compliance with EPR (Extended Producer Responsibility) norms

Micro-Dust as Fuel

  • Steam generation using micro-dust reduces dependence on conventional fuels and further lowers emissions

State-of-the-Art Sustainability Infrastructure

By seamlessly integrating innovative technology with eco-conscious practices, Sintex Yarns exemplifies
sustainability at every level, from production to product.

Zero Discharge Effluent Treatment Plant

Processing 6,000 KLD of wastewater, ensuring no discharge

Reverse Osmosis (RO) Plant

Achieving 92% water recovery, significantly lowering water wastage.

Multiple Effect Evaporators (MEE)

Advanced wastewater treatment for a complete, eco-friendly water cycle.

Sintex Industries Limited is a leading Indian textile manufacturer, renowned for premium-quality yarns like 100% cotton & blends, 100% Linen & blends, value-added, and sustainable options. We also produce 100% bleached absorbent cotton which is used for medical and hygiene product applications. Serving global and domestic markets, Sintex is committed to innovation, quality, and reliability.
