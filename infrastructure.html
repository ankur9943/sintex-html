Infrastructure

We have transformed the Indian spinning industry by establishing the country’s largest spinning plant, featuring over 6.73 lakh spindles at a single location, spread across 523 acres—a first in the history of Indian textiles. We have successfully commissioned the entire spindle capacity and made the plant fully operational within just three years, setting a new benchmark for the Indian spinning sector.
