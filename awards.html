Awards

Certificate of Appreciation from Govt. Of Gujarat–Jan’25

We are proud to have received a Certificate of Appreciation from Ms. Gargi Jain (IAS), Director of Employment & Training and Director of Skill Development at DET Gujarat. This recognition celebrates our efforts in empowering and uplifting the youth of Gujarat.It strengthens our commitment to nurturing talent, fostering sustainable growth, and creating meaningful opportunities for a brighter future.

Honoured to Be Named a Top Supplier by Welspun Living Limited–Dec’24

We are honoured to be recognized as a Top Supplier by Welspun Living Limited at the Welspun Partner Summit 2024. Our Marketing Head, Mr. Naveen Sharma, received the prestigious Panellist Award from Mrs. Dipali Goenka and Mr. Ashok Kumar Joshi. This recognition reflects our commitment to quality, innovation, and strong partnerships, and motivates us to keep driving excellence in the textileindustries.

GUJARAT BEST EMPLOYER BRAND AWARD 2018

A company is better defined by the satisfaction of employees who work for them. We at Sintex are proud to associate ourselves with the growth track of our employees. Sintex Group has secured the title of being the Best Employer Brand Awards 2018, for its enthusiastic approach and active thinking beyond imagination

Sintex Industries Limited is a leading Indian textile manufacturer, renowned for premium-quality yarns like 100% cotton & blends, 100% Linen & blends, value-added, and sustainable options. We also produce 100% bleached absorbent cotton which is used for medical and hygiene product applications. Serving global and domestic markets, Sintex is committed to innovation, quality, and reliability.
