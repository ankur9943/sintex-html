Led by a highly motivated and experienced management, the Sintex group’s leadership team represents the company’s strategy and expertise.

Board of Directors

Shri Achuthan Siddharth

Non-Executive - Independent Director (Chairman)

Shri Achuthan Siddharth is a Commerce and Law graduate from the Mumbai University, a fellow member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India and an associate member of the Institute of Company Secretaries of India. He was associated with Deloitte, Haskins & Sells for over 4 decades and served as Partner for 33 years. He has vast and varied experience in the field.

Shri Shiv Kumar Bhardwaj

Non-Executive - Independent Director

Shri Shiv Kumar Bhardwaj has more than thirty five years of experience of working in various wings of the Government of India. He has held important posts like Chief Commissioner of Customs, Central Excise and Service Tax, Mumbai and Vadodara, and Joint Secretary in the Ministry of Defence. He retired as the Member, Central Board of Indirect Taxes, the apex body administering GST, Central Excise, Customs and Service Tax laws. He has vast experience of tax policy planning, budget making and administration in the Revenue Department. He underwent training at Harvard University, USA, in Fiscal Policy and Value Added Taxes. He takes keen interest in matters related to tax reforms, ease of doing business and conflict avoidance.

Shri Antony Joseph Leo

Whole Time Director

Shri. Antony Joseph Leo is a textile technologist, having an experience of over 38 years in the field of Textile spinning spanning across all functions of Manufacturing, quality and Maintenance along with Planning experience in composite Mills. He has been associated with Sintex since 2021.

Shri Asokan Arumugam

Non-Executive Director

Shri Asokan Arumugam has more than 35 years of experience in Treasury Corporate & Investment Banking, Retail & SME lending and Asset Reconstruction Business and he also held position of Treasurer, had credit approval discretions in Corporate Banking and set up SME & Mid Corporate businesses with all India coverage.

Smt. Savithri Parekh

Non-Executive Director

Smt. Savithri Parekh is B.Com., LL.B. and a fellow member of the Institute of Company Secretaries of India, New Delhi. She has 28 years of rich and varied experience in the fields of legal and secretarial. Prior to joining Reliance Group, she was Sr. Vice President, Legal & Company Secretary, of Pidilite Industries Limited for 9 years. She has been a guest faculty at IIM Kolkata for over 10 years and has also authored three books on the Companies Act, 2013 and on Listing Regulations.
