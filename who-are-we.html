Who Are We?

A Benchmark in Textile Excellence

Redefining textile standards with advanced technology and global expertise

Sintex Yarns has redefined the Indian textile landscape, emerging as a symbol of innovation, scale, and sustainability. Located in Gujarat, the largest cotton-producing state in India, Sintex operates India’s largest spinning plant, with over 6.73 lakh spindles spread across 523 acres. This remarkable achievement has established Sintex as a leader in the industry.

Since its establishment in 2014, Sintex Yarns has continuously created value by leveraging the economy of scale to deliver competitive, high-quality products, and by expanding into international markets through strong customer partnerships. Our integration with the Reliance Group (Fortune Global 500) in 2023 marked a significant leap in capability, credibility, and global reach combining Sintex’s world-class spinning expertise with Reliance’s unmatched resources. Together, we drive innovation, sustainability, and supply chain excellence across every link of our value chain.

With exports to 50+ countries, a strong global distribution network, and proximity to major ports such as Pipavav and Mundra, we ensure efficient logistics and timely deliveries. Our warehousing support at Lunsapur and Tirupur further enhances operational agility — enabling seamless service and consistent quality for customers across the world.

The Foundation of Our Success

Our Vision

To become a global leader in textiles, inspiring innovation, sustainability, and inclusive growth while delivering unparalleled quality and delighting customers worldwide.

Our Mission

To operate as a world class textile organization, providing high-quality products and exceptional customer service through state-of-the-art technology, innovative practices, and collaborative partnerships. We are committed to addressing evolving needs, fostering progress, and upholding our responsibilities as good corporate citizens.

Our Core Values

Customer Value

We believe the customer is the cornerstone of our existence and the ultimate guarantee of our future. Every action we take is dedicated to delighting our customers, ensuring satisfaction each time and always.

Ownership Mindset

The success and reputation of the company are paramount. An ownership mindset inspires accountability, purpose, and accomplishment. This commitment ensures we uphold the highest standards of safety, sustainability, and environmental responsibility.

Respect

We understand that respect is the foundation of trust. We value the perspectives of all stakeholders, acknowledging that differences exist but must always be met with mutual respect.

Integrity

Integrity is at the core of everything we do. We uphold honesty and transparency in all actions, building trust with stakeholders and complying with laws and regulations. We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards to earn and maintain this trust.

One Team

We achieve more together. By prioritizing the team’s success over individual accomplishments, we foster collaboration and trust. We are committed to building each other’s capabilities and delivering on our collective goals.

Excellence

Excellence drives our every effort. We are committed to continuous improvement, striving to make everything we do better. Guided by our higher purpose and ideals, we consistently pursue excellence in spirit and action.

Group Companies

United by shared values and a collective vision

We became a part of the Reliance Group (Fortune Global 500) in 2023, marking a major leap in capability and trust. This powerful integration brings together Sintex’s world-class spinning expertise with Reliance’s unmatched global reach and resources thereby driving innovation, sustainability, and supply chain excellence.

BVM overseas Ltd (Wholly owned subsidiary of Sintex Industries Limited)

BVM Overseas Limited is the trading arm of Sintex Industries Limited for its domestic and international markets. The Company currently trades in various types yarns such as cotton yarn linen yarn, blended yarn etc.

Sintex Industries Limited is a leading Indian textile manufacturer, renowned for premium-quality yarns like 100% cotton & blends, 100% Linen & blends, value-added, and sustainable options. We also produce 100% bleached absorbent cotton which is used for medical and hygiene product applications. Serving global and domestic markets, Sintex is committed to innovation, quality, and reliability.
