100% Bleached Absorbent Cotton

Sintex is a leading manufacturer of absorbent cotton (Surgical Cotton) in India. Known for its high absorbency, our cotton is widely used in the medical industry for dressings and other applications. Our purified cotton is free from residual chemicals and toxins, making it ideal for Surgical applications and feminine hygiene products like sanitary pads, Cotton Buds, Tampons and Cotton Rolls. We ensure our cotton is 100%, pure, and meets international quality standards through automated and hygienic manufacturing processes.
